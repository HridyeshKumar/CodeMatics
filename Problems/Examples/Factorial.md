# Factorial

The factorial of a non-negative integer 
𝑛
n is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to 
𝑛

 It is denoted by 𝑛! (read as "n factorial").

> 
>𝑛 = ∏n = n × (n-1) × (n-2) . . . . .(n-(n-1))

As the call are Recursive,The base condition is :

> 0! = 1
